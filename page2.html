About Us

Solution Media and InfoTech Limited is a Professional Interactive, Creative and Hi-tech multimedia company. Which uses informative presentation such as light, sound, graphics and laser light effect e.t.c to achieve an excellent result. We make use of an attractive and technological advanced approach for all our clients through an optically pleasant and impressive technological environment. We endorse the principles of corporate responsibility through our sustainable-development strategies and the policies affecting our employees and the community.

Our Philosophy is to create an exciting and new spectacular entertainment experience for our clients by bringing together cutting-edge product with latest technological contents and services. We are committed to developing a wide range of fresh ideas and multimedia services that challenge the way people access and enjoy entertainment, by ensuring synergy between businesses and their respective organization.

We are constantly striving to create an exciting new world of entertainment that can be experienced on a variety of our different services. We believe in the "WOW" factor for every project we undertake. Our team's diversity and wealth of experience, combined with our abundant creativity, ensure our clients receive a professional and reliable service with the best possible end product to represent their business.
